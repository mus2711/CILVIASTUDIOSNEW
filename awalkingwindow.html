A WALKING WINDOW

A conceptual prototype of an interactive window, built and programmed with arduino and hardware sensors.

By: Mustafa Al Quraishi, for Imperial College London.

Problem: What if, your window blocked out the sun when itâ€™s too bright, opened and closed at just the right temperature, was always where you wanted it to be?

Solution: prototype was built out of cardboard and consisted of a 3D printed rack and pinion set, stepper motors, DC motor, ultrasonic, temperature and capacative light sensors to control the motions.

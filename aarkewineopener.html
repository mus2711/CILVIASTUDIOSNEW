AARKE WINE OPENER

An Electric Wine Bottle Opener for Users with Weak Wrists and Hand Tremors.

By: Mustafa Al Quraishi, Samantha Foong and Michelle Lee for Imperial College London. With no affiliation to Cilvia Studios or Aarke.

Problem: For those with limited dexterity and strength, the use of these mechanical levers and other mechanisms to open a wine bottle requires high strength, finger dexterity and leverage in order to open the bottle.

Solution: an opportunity to offer a wine bottle opener with electric power assistance to allow users to easily enjoy opening wine, whilst mainting its allore.
